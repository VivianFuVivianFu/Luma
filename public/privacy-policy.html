Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Effective Date: January 2025

Designed for Australian and New Zealand Privacy Laws

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Luma, your privacy and data security are fundamental to everything we do. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (provided during registration)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Authentication data (securely hashed passwords)

Conversation Data

  • Text messages exchanged with Luma
  • Voice recordings during voice calls (processed and stored securely)
  • Session metadata (timestamps, conversation context)

Technical Information

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location data
  • Usage analytics and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI Companion Services: To deliver personalized emotional support and conversations
  • Improve Our Service: To enhance Luma's responses and user experience
  • Maintain Account Security: To protect your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our obligations under Australian and New Zealand law

Data Security & Encryption

Bank-Level Security

All your conversations are encrypted using AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by financial institutions.

  • End-to-end encryption for all message transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with automated backups
  • SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication support

Data Storage & Location

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, with primary data centers located in regions that ensure optimal performance for Australian and New Zealand users. We ensure that all data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and maintain the same level of protection regardless of location.

Data Sovereignty: We respect your right to know where your data is stored and processed, and we maintain transparency about our data handling practices.

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Control

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Download your conversation history
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and all associated data

Privacy Controls

  • Opt-out of data processing for service improvement
  • Restrict or object to certain data uses
  • Data portability - export your information
  • Notification of data breaches

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Conversation Data: Stored until you delete your account or request removal
  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days
  • Technical Logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Deleted Data: Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

Third-Party Services

We use carefully selected third-party services to provide Luma's functionality:

Anthropic Claude API

AI conversation processing - data encrypted in transit, not stored by Anthropic

Supabase

Secure database and authentication services

ElevenLabs

Voice synthesis for audio responses - data processed securely

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and provide core functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or share data with advertising networks.

Cookie Types:

  • Authentication cookies (essential for login)
  • Session management cookies
  • Security and fraud prevention cookies

Children's Privacy

Luma is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.
